Featured Manufacturing AI Unique Problem

StockSentry: AI Warehouse Stock Reconciliation

Large manufacturing facilities like BrewCo Manufacturing lose millions monthly to untracked stock variances. Everything is recorded on paper so small daily losses of 1 to 2 crates get ignored as acceptable loss. By the time monthly reconciliation catches the discrepancy, the trail is cold and the money is gone. StockSentry replaces paper shift records with a real-time digital system that catches every variance the moment it happens, classifies the likely cause using AI reasoning, and detects recurring patterns across the last 30 shifts per brand. The monthly reconciliation report that previously took 2 full days now runs in under 1 second.

Healthcare AI WhatsApp

MediFlow: AI WhatsApp Clinic Receptionist

Kenyan clinics lose KES 400,000 or more monthly to no-shows and spend over 2 hours daily on manual confirmation calls. MediFlow handles appointment booking, reminders, and clinic enquiries through WhatsApp so the receptionist only deals with exceptions. Emergency symptoms trigger a chain of thought assessment that escalates to a human immediately. For any medical question, the system books an appointment rather than attempting to answer it. Every message and decision is logged to PostgreSQL.

ClearVet: Client Evaluation Pipeline

AI automation agencies spend hours manually screening client enquiries and most are not a good fit. ClearVet automates the entire screening process. A potential client fills a Typeform, Claude evaluates them across 6 dimensions, a scored report appears in Slack with Approve/Reject buttons, and the decision is logged to PostgreSQL permanently.
